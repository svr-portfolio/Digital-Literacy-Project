# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s
- Be respectful while posting content  
- Use strong and secure passwords  
- Share useful and positive information  
- Think before posting anything online  
- Keep your profile professional  

## Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information  
- Do not use offensive or inappropriate language  
- Do not spread false or unverified information  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Do not overshare private details online
