## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s
- Be respectful and polite in your social media posts or comments  
- Always use unique and strong passwords for your social media accounts  
- Enable the two-factor feature for your social media accounts  
- Share only useful and positive information  
- Always think before posting something online because it might last forever  
- Maintain a professional profile, especially if you are using LinkedIn  
- Always verify the information before posting it to avoid the spread of false information  
- Be respectful of other people’s privacy and always ask before posting something related to them  
- Be in control of your social media by managing the visibility of your posts  
- Always report inappropriate information if the need arises  
- Always engage in a constructive discussion  
- Always keep your social media apps and other related software up to date.
  
## Don’ts
- Avoid sharing personal information like passwords, OTPs, or bank account numbers  
- Avoid using offensive or abusive language  
- Avoid spreading false or misleading information  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links  
- Avoid oversharing personal information like location or daily routines  
- Avoid cyberbullying, trolling, or harassing people  
- Avoid accepting friend requests from unknown or suspicious accounts  
- Avoid downloading unknown or suspicious content  
- Avoid posting anything that could harm your reputation or future prospects  
- Avoid ignoring features related to online safety and security  
- Avoid impersonating people or using fake accounts  
- Avoid sharing copyrighted material without permission
