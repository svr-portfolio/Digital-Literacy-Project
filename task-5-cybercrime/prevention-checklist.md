# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist for Students

- Never share your OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links received through messages, emails, or social media  
- Always verify payment requests before accepting them, especially on UPI apps  
- Do not scan QR codes from unknown sources or strangers  
- Use only trusted apps for online payments and downloads  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on your accounts for extra security  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking, payments, or logging into important accounts  
- Regularly check your bank account and transaction history  
- Use strong and unique passwords for your email, social media, and academic accounts  
- Do not share personal information like phone number, address, or college details publicly  
- Be cautious of fake job offers, internship messages, or prize notifications  
- Always check website links before entering login or payment details  
- Log out of accounts when using shared or public devices (labs, libraries, etc.)  
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security updates  

## Reporting Cybercrime

- Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
