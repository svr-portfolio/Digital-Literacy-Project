# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist for Students

## Do’s
- Always use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts to avoid unauthorized access  
- Enable 2-factor authentication for your accounts to add an extra layer of security  
- Always check the payment requests before accepting them in UPI or banking apps  
- Only use trusted and official apps for payment, downloads, and communication  
- Always keep your mobile phone, laptop, and apps up-to-date with the latest security updates  
- Always keep an eye on your bank accounts and transactions to identify any suspicious activity  
- Always change the privacy settings of your social media accounts to control access to your information  
- Always be cautious before sharing your information online and think twice before posting something online  
- Always save important contact numbers like the bank helpline number in your phone for emergency use  
- Always log out of your accounts if you are using a shared or public device  
- Always keep yourself informed about the common cyber scams and fraud techniques  
- Always use antivirus or security software to protect your device from malware

## Don’ts  
- Do not share your OTP, PIN, passwords, or bank account information with anyone, even if they say they are officials  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links sent through messages, emails, or social media  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes, as it may result in money being deducted from your account  
- Do not access the internet using public Wi-Fi for transactions or logging into your account  
- Do not accept friend requests or messages from unknown people  
- Do not download apps or media from unknown or unofficial websites  
- Do not share your phone number, address, or college name with unknown people  
- Do not ignore security alerts or notifications  
- Do not panic if you receive an urgent message; always verify it first  
- Do not keep your account information or login credentials with unknown people  
- Do not keep the same password for multiple accounts  
- Do not allow unknown people to access your screen  

## Reporting Cybercrime  
- Report cybercrime at https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Contact the Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930  
- Inform your bank if you suspect an unauthorized transaction from your account.
